## Delivery standards

- Supply AVIF and WebP with JPG fallback.
- Keep hero source files at least 1920 pixels wide.
- Create separate desktop and mobile focal crops where needed.
- Record photographer, copyright owner, license and expiry.
- Obtain and retain contributor/model releases when identifiable people are central.
- Do not use unverified AI imagery as documentary evidence of a destination, community, ceremony or species.
- Write factual alt text; do not stuff keywords.
- Test Largest Contentful Paint after image integration.

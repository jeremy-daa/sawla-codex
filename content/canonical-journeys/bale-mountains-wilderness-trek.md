# Bale Mountains Wilderness Trek

**Canonical:** `https://www.sawlatours.com/tours-by-experience/ethiopia-adventure-tours/bale-mountains-wilderness-trek`
**Duration:** 11 days / 10 nights
**Route:** Addis Ababa – Dinsho – Web Valley – Sanetti Plateau – Rafu – Harenna Forest – Addis Ababa
**Operating status:** Conditional departure — trail, park and support logistics required
**Difficulty:** Challenging
**Best months:** October to March; other windows by rainfall and trail assessment

## Across Afroalpine plateau, volcanic formations and Harenna forest

Bale is Ethiopia’s most varied highland trek: grassland around Dinsho, the Web Valley, the Afroalpine Sanetti Plateau, volcanic rock at Rafu and the descent toward Harenna Forest.

The route is supported by local guides, camp staff and pack animals where appropriate. Wildlife is an important part of the journey, but wolf, nyala and Bale monkey sightings are never promised.


## Why this journey works

- Crosses several ecological zones in one supported trek
- Combines walking with endemic-wildlife interpretation
- Finishes in forest rather than retracing the same plateau route

## Highlights

- Dinsho wildlife and acclimatization
- Web Valley walking
- Sanetti Plateau and Ethiopian wolf habitat
- Rafu volcanic formations
- Harenna Forest descent
- Mountain nyala and endemic birds when present

## Frequently asked questions

### How cold does Bale become?

Nights on the plateau can be below freezing, while days can be sunny and exposed. The equipment list is built for both.

### Will we see Ethiopian wolves?

Bale is the best place to look, but sightings depend on current distribution, weather and field conditions.

### Can the route be done without camping?

A shorter lodge-based version can use Dinsho, Goba or other current bases, but it will not reproduce the full traverse.

### Is this a fixed departure or a private journey?

It is a private sample journey rather than a scheduled group departure. Sawla adjusts the dates, sequence, accommodation, pace, guide profile and activity level around the traveler and current operating conditions.

### What is included in the final quotation?

The final proposal states every inclusion precisely. It normally covers agreed accommodation, private transport, guiding, listed visits and operational support; international flights, insurance, visas, personal spending and tips are normally separate unless stated otherwise.

### Can this itinerary be customized?

Yes. Extra nights, slower pacing, accommodation changes, specialist guides, domestic-flight alternatives and regional extensions can be built into the final journey.

## From Awash drylands to Bale highlands, Yabello endemics and southern lakes

Ethiopia’s elevation and habitat diversity make it one of Africa’s strongest endemic-rich birding destinations. This route moves through dry acacia country, Rift lakes, Afroalpine plateau, forest, southern bushland and escarpment.

The program is target-led but not checklist-driven. Daily plans adapt to weather, current sightings and mixed-interest needs, and species totals are never guaranteed.


## Why this journey works

- Covers the principal southern endemic habitats in one route
- Allows repeated Bale and Yabello field sessions
- Balances target birds with mammals, landscape and comfortable recovery stops

## Highlights

- Awash and nearby dry-country birding
- Rift Valley waterbirds
- Bale endemic and near-endemic targets
- Sanetti Plateau raptors and Ethiopian wolf possibility
- Yabello bushcrow and white-tailed swallow range
- Arba Minch escarpment and lake habitats

## Frequently asked questions

### How many bird species will we see?

No responsible operator guarantees a total. The route is designed for strong endemic potential, but weather, skill, season and chance affect the list.

### Is this suitable for a non-birding partner?

Yes with careful design. Scenic, wildlife and cultural alternatives can be built around the key early-morning sessions.

### Do you use playback?

Only selectively and according to the specialist guide’s ethical judgment, avoiding stress, breeding disruption and repeated pressure.

### Is this a fixed departure or a private journey?

It is a private sample journey rather than a scheduled group departure. Sawla adjusts the dates, sequence, accommodation, pace, guide profile and activity level around the traveler and current operating conditions.

### What is included in the final quotation?

The final proposal states every inclusion precisely. It normally covers agreed accommodation, private transport, guiding, listed visits and operational support; international flights, insurance, visas, personal spending and tips are normally separate unless stated otherwise.

### Can this itinerary be customized?

Yes. Extra nights, slower pacing, accommodation changes, specialist guides, domestic-flight alternatives and regional extensions can be built into the final journey.

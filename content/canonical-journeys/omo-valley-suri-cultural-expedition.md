**Route:** Addis Ababa – Jimma – Mizan Teferi – Kibish/Suri – Jinka – Turmi – Lower Omo – Addis Ababa

## Two distinct southern regions connected through time, consent and expedition logistics

This expedition combines two culturally distinct regions that are often incorrectly treated as one route. The Suri sector in the southwest requires its own road, camp, local-coordinator and permission plan; the Lower Omo sector uses Jinka and Turmi as separate operational bases.

Four field days in the Suri area create time for ordinary life, cattle culture, markets or ceremonies when genuinely occurring. The Lower Omo module then adds different communities and river landscapes without turning the journey into a race.


## Why this journey works

- Treats Suri and Lower Omo as separate cultural and logistical systems
- Includes enough field time to move beyond a single portrait stop
- Uses mobile-camp and 4×4 planning where accommodation is limited

## Highlights

- Southwest road journey through Jimma and Mizan
- Four Kibish-area field days with local coordination
- Suri cattle, landscape and daily-life context
- Donga or other ceremonies only when genuinely occurring and appropriate
- Ari, Hamar, Kara or Dassanech visits in the Lower Omo
- Transparent photography and community protocols

## Frequently asked questions

### How much time is spent with Suri hosts?

The sample includes four full field days around Kibish. That time is necessary because access, local activity and trust cannot be compressed into a roadside stop.

### Is Donga guaranteed?

No. Donga is a community event with its own timing and decisions. It is included only when genuinely occurring, locally approved and safe.

### What accommodation is used in Kibish?

The final proposal specifies the current option, which may be a mobile camp or very simple local accommodation. Sawla explains power, washing, toilets, food and privacy before booking.

### Is this a fixed departure or a private journey?

It is a private sample journey rather than a scheduled group departure. Sawla adjusts the dates, sequence, accommodation, pace, guide profile and activity level around the traveler and current operating conditions.

### What is included in the final quotation?

The final proposal states every inclusion precisely. It normally covers agreed accommodation, private transport, guiding, listed visits and operational support; international flights, insurance, visas, personal spending and tips are normally separate unless stated otherwise.

### Can this itinerary be customized?

Yes. Extra nights, slower pacing, accommodation changes, specialist guides, domestic-flight alternatives and regional extensions can be built into the final journey.

**Route:** Addis Ababa – Gondar – Simien Mountains trek – Gondar – Lalibela – Addis Ababa

## Four supported walking days, Gondar’s royal city and Lalibela’s rock-hewn churches

This journey gives the Simiens enough time to feel like a trek rather than a scenic day trip, then connects the landscape to Gondar and Lalibela. Four walking days can be adjusted between lodge, fixed camp and mobile support.

Altitude, cold and uneven trails are the main challenges. Daily distances are agreed in advance, and local conditions determine the exact camps and viewpoints.

## Frequently asked questions

### How fit do I need to be?

You should be comfortable walking four to six hours on consecutive days at altitude. The route can be shortened, but elevation remains demanding.

### Do I carry my main luggage?

Normally no. Pack animals or support arrangements carry camp and main baggage, while you carry water, layers and personal essentials.

### Can the trek use lodges instead of tents?

A lodge-based version is possible for selected routes, subject to current operation and availability.

### Is this a fixed departure or a private journey?

It is a private sample journey rather than a scheduled group departure. Sawla adjusts the dates, sequence, accommodation, pace, guide profile and activity level around the traveler and current operating conditions.

### What is included in the final quotation?

The final proposal states every inclusion precisely. It normally covers agreed accommodation, private transport, guiding, listed visits and operational support; international flights, insurance, visas, personal spending and tips are normally separate unless stated otherwise.

### Can this itinerary be customized?

Yes. Extra nights, slower pacing, accommodation changes, specialist guides, domestic-flight alternatives and regional extensions can be built into the final journey.

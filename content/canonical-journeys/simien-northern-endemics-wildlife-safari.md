## Geladas, highland birds and escarpment landscapes in a focused northern safari

This focused safari is built for travelers who want more than a one-day Simien viewpoint but do not need a full trek. Two or three field days allow patient gelada observation, endemic and highland birding, raptor watching and landscape photography.

Walia ibex is possible but never guaranteed. The route can use a lodge or carefully specified camp depending on current operation and comfort expectations.

## Day-by-day itinerary

### Day 1 — Arrive Addis Ababa

Arrival and a trekking briefing. Meet your Sawla representative, transfer privately and review the route, current operations, baggage, health considerations and the pace planned for the days ahead.

**Overnight:** Addis Ababa

### Day 2 — Fly to Gondar

Fly north, with an afternoon at Fasil Ghebbi. Early and late sessions are balanced with realistic transfers, and playback is used only selectively under the guide’s ethical judgment.

**Overnight:** Gondar

### Day 3 — Drive to Sankaber

Enter the park and trek to the first camp. The day combines target-led searching with broader habitat interpretation so the route remains rewarding even when a key species is not located.

**Overnight:** Sankaber or Simien Mountains

### Day 4 — Trek to Geech

A full trekking day along the escarpment, with gelada encounters. The specialist guide adjusts target habitats to weather, seasonal movement and recent records; totals and individual species remain possibilities rather than promises.

**Overnight:** Geech camp

### Day 5 — Geech to Chenek

The most scenic section, beneath lammergeiers and within reach of Walia ibex. Early and late sessions are balanced with realistic transfers, and playback is used only selectively under the guide’s ethical judgment.

**Overnight:** Chenek or Simien Mountains

### Day 6 — Fly to Addis and Depart

Fly to Addis and connect onward. The final transfer is planned with a protected connection buffer; a further Addis Ababa night is recommended when domestic and international tickets are separate.

**Overnight:** Departure or Addis Ababa


## Frequently asked questions

### Will we see Walia ibex?

They are a target, not a guarantee. Current ranger information and walking ability determine the search area.

### Is this a birding tour or mammal safari?

It is a mixed northern-endemics safari. Dedicated birders can add earlier starts and target-specific guiding.

### Can the trip avoid camping?

Yes when suitable lodge or town accommodation is operating, though more daily driving may be required.

### Is this a fixed departure or a private journey?

It is a private sample journey rather than a scheduled group departure. Sawla adjusts the dates, sequence, accommodation, pace, guide profile and activity level around the traveler and current operating conditions.

### What is included in the final quotation?

The final proposal states every inclusion precisely. It normally covers agreed accommodation, private transport, guiding, listed visits and operational support; international flights, insurance, visas, personal spending and tips are normally separate unless stated otherwise.

### Can this itinerary be customized?

Yes. Extra nights, slower pacing, accommodation changes, specialist guides, domestic-flight alternatives and regional extensions can be built into the final journey.

**Duration:** 11 days / 10 nights
**Route:** Addis Ababa – Rift Valley – Arba Minch – Dorze – Konso – Jinka – Turmi – Addis Ababa
**Operating status:** Conditional departure — southern road and community review required
**Difficulty:** Moderate
**Best months:** October to March; selected green-season departures by road assessment

## Rift lakes, Dorze highlands, Konso terraces and Lower Omo at a more human pace

This journey uses the landscapes and societies north of the Lower Omo as part of the story rather than as transfer stops. Rift Valley lakes, Dorze highland livelihoods and Konso’s terraced cultural landscape create context before the hotter lowlands.

Two-night stays and fewer community visits reduce the pressure to collect images. The result works particularly well for active older travelers, first-time visitors and people interested in food, farming, architecture and present-day change.


## Why this journey works

- Adds Dorze and Konso rather than flying directly into the lowlands
- Uses more two-night stays and less daily packing
- Works well without transactional portrait photography

## Frequently asked questions

### Why choose this instead of the eight-day Omo journey?

It adds Dorze and Konso, uses more two-night stays and gives better cultural and landscape context before Lower Omo.

### Is the route suitable for older travelers?

Yes for many active older travelers, provided they are comfortable with road time and uneven paths. Walking and visits can be adjusted.

### Can the tour avoid transactional portrait photography?

Yes. The route works well for travelers who prefer conversation, landscape, food, farming and observation. Photography is optional and permission-based.

### Is this a fixed departure or a private journey?

It is a private sample journey rather than a scheduled group departure. Sawla adjusts the dates, sequence, accommodation, pace, guide profile and activity level around the traveler and current operating conditions.

### What is included in the final quotation?

The final proposal states every inclusion precisely. It normally covers agreed accommodation, private transport, guiding, listed visits and operational support; international flights, insurance, visas, personal spending and tips are normally separate unless stated otherwise.

### Can this itinerary be customized?

Yes. Extra nights, slower pacing, accommodation changes, specialist guides, domestic-flight alternatives and regional extensions can be built into the final journey.

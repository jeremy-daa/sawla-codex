# ITINERARY 1: CLASSIC NORTHERN ETHIOPIA — 10 DAYS

**Slug:** classic-northern-ethiopia-10-days
**Style:** Heritage Pilgrimage / Historic & Cultural
**Duration:** 10 days
**Price:** From $2,800 per person (2 travelers)
**Best for:** First-time Ethiopia visitors, history lovers, cultural travelers
**Best months:** October–February

## Overview

The Classic Northern Ethiopia circuit takes you through four thousand years of African civilization — from the ancient Aksumite capital at Gondar through the island monasteries of Lake Tana to the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela. This is the journey that defines Ethiopia for most international travelers, and with good reason: nowhere else in Africa can you experience this depth of living historical culture in such a concentrated geography.

## Day-by-Day

**Day 1: Arrive Addis Ababa**
Evening arrival at Bole International Airport. Transfer to hotel in Addis Ababa (Bole district, close to airport). Welcome dinner and briefing from your Sawla Tours specialist on the journey ahead.

*Overnight: Addis Ababa (recommended: Hyatt Regency, Radisson Blu, or similar)*

**Day 2: Addis Ababa city day**
Morning visit to the National Museum of Ethiopia — home to Lucy, one of the most significant paleoanthropological discoveries in history. Afternoon: a guided Merkato visit, Holy Trinity Cathedral. Evening: traditional Ethiopian dinner with coffee ceremony.

*Overnight: Addis Ababa*

**Day 3: Fly Addis → Bahir Dar · Lake Tana monasteries**
Early morning domestic flight to Bahir Dar (~1 hour). Boat excursion to the island monasteries of Lake Tana — Ura Kidane Mihret and Debre Damo are the most accessible and impressive. Afternoon free on the lakeshore.

*Overnight: Bahir Dar (recommended: Abay Minch Lodge, Kuriftu Resort, or similar)*

**Day 4: Blue Nile Falls · Drive to Gondar**
Morning visit to Tis Abay (Blue Nile Falls) — most spectacular during or just after the rainy season. Drive north to Gondar (approximately 3 hours). Afternoon arrival and first views of the Royal Enclosure.

*Overnight: Gondar (recommended: Fasil Lodge, Goha Hotel, or similar)*

**Day 5: Gondar — Fasil Ghebbi and Debre Berhan Selassie**
Full day in Gondar. Morning: Fasil Ghebbi Royal Enclosure — the castles of six successive emperors, the library, the chancellery. Afternoon: Debre Berhan Selassie Church, with its extraordinary painted ceiling of cherub faces. Optional: Fasilides Bath.

*Overnight: Gondar*

**Day 6: Fly Gondar → Lalibela**
Morning domestic flight to Lalibela (~1 hour). Afternoon: initial orientation walk with your guide and first visit to the southern churches (Bete Giyorgis, Bete Maryam). Time the afternoon visit for the best light.

*Overnight: Lalibela (recommended: Limalimo Lodge, Mountain View Hotel, or Ben Abeba)*

**Day 7: Lalibela — full day, northern churches**
Full day dedicated to the northern church group. Bete Medhane Alem (the largest), Bete Maryam, Bete Meskel, Bete Denagel. Morning liturgy if attending early service. Afternoon: the connecting trenches and tunnels between churches.

*Overnight: Lalibela*

**Day 8: Lalibela — Asheton Maryam and eastern churches**
Morning: walk or mule ride to Asheton Maryam monastery above the main complex — 45-minute ascent, extraordinary views. Afternoon: eastern church group — Bete Amanuel (most finely carved), Bete Abba Libanos, Bete Merkorios.

*Overnight: Lalibela*

**Day 9: Fly Lalibela → Addis Ababa · farewell dinner**
Morning flight back to Addis Ababa. Afternoon free for final shopping (Addis is the best place for Ethiopian textiles, art, and coffee). Farewell dinner with your guide at a recommended restaurant.

*Overnight: Addis Ababa*

**Day 10: Depart**
Transfer to Bole International Airport for international departure.

# ITINERARY 2: LALIBELA, GONDAR & TIGRAY DEEP DIVE — 14 DAYS

**Slug:** lalibela-gondar-tigray-14-days
**Style:** Heritage Pilgrimage / Historic & Cultural
**Duration:** 14 days
**Price:** From $3,800 per person (2 travelers)

## Overview

This itinerary adds Tigray's extraordinary cliff-face rock churches to the classic northern circuit — making it the most comprehensive historic Ethiopia journey available. Tigray's churches predate Lalibela by several centuries and are, architecturally, even more dramatic — accessible only by rope and ladder on near-vertical cliff faces, containing frescos unchanged for 800 years.

## Day-by-Day (Summary)

**Days 1–2:** Addis Ababa
**Day 3:** Fly to Mekelle (Tigray gateway)
**Day 4:** Gheralta landscape and first cliff churches (Abuna Yemata Guh)
**Day 5:** Further Tigray churches — Maryam Korkor, Daniel Korkor
**Day 6:** Drive Tigray → Axum (Ancient Aksumite capital)
**Day 7:** Axum — stelae, Church of St Mary of Zion, ancient tombs
**Day 8:** Fly Axum → Gondar
**Days 9–10:** Gondar (Fasil Ghebbi, Debre Berhan Selassie)
**Day 11:** Fly Gondar → Lalibela
**Days 12–13:** Lalibela (all churches, Asheton Maryam)
**Day 14:** Fly → Addis, depart

# ITINERARY 3: WOLF & GELADA — BALE & SIMIEN WILDLIFE CIRCUIT — 12 DAYS

**Slug:** bale-simien-wolf-gelada-12-days
**Style:** Wildlife & Birding
**Duration:** 12 days
**Price:** From $3,600 per person (2 travelers)
**Best for:** Wildlife enthusiasts, naturalists, wildlife photographers
**Specialist note:** Ethiopian wolf sightings are probable on properly timed visits to Bale; gelada encounters at Simien are almost guaranteed.

## Overview

This is the definitive Ethiopian endemic wildlife circuit — combining the two most important wildlife destinations in the country. The Bale Mountains (Ethiopian wolf, mountain nyala, giant mole-rat) and the Simien Mountains (gelada monkey, Walia ibex, lammergeier vulture) are very different landscapes that together provide access to the full range of Ethiopia's most remarkable endemic mammals.

## Day-by-Day (Summary)

**Day 1:** Arrive Addis Ababa
**Day 2:** Fly Addis → Robe; drive to Bale Mountains / Dinsho
**Day 3:** Bale — Dinsho area, mountain nyala, Bale monkey in Harenna Forest
**Day 4:** Full day Sanetti Plateau — Ethiopian wolf focus, giant mole-rat, afroalpine birds
**Day 5:** Second plateau day (Ethiopian wolf behavioural photography)
**Day 6:** Descend Harenna Forest; fly Robe → Addis
**Day 7:** Fly Addis → Gondar; drive Simien Mountains, Sankaber
**Day 8:** Trek Sankaber → Chenek escarpment; first gelada encounter
**Day 9:** Full day Chenek — geladas, Walia ibex, lammergeier
**Day 10:** Return Debark; drive Gondar; visit Fasil Ghebbi
**Day 11:** Fly Gondar → Lalibela; afternoon church orientation
**Day 12:** Lalibela morning; fly → Addis; depart

# ITINERARY 4: OMO VALLEY COMPLETE — 10 DAYS

**Slug:** omo-valley-complete-10-days
**Style:** Tribal & Cultural Encounters
**Duration:** 10 days
**Price:** From $3,200 per person (2 travelers)

## Overview

The Omo Valley is one of the most culturally complex destinations in Africa. This 10-day itinerary moves through the main communities of the lower Omo — Hamar, Karo, Mursi, Daasanach — spending sufficient time in each area to experience genuine encounters rather than drive-by visits.

## Day-by-Day (Summary)

**Day 1:** Fly Addis → Arba Minch; drive to Konso
**Day 2:** Konso World Heritage Site (terraced farming, waga sculptures)
**Day 3:** Drive south to Jinka; Mago National Park vicinity, Mursi community
**Day 4:** Mursi extended visit; drive to Turmi (Hamar territory)
**Day 5:** Hamar market day at Dimeka; evening Hamar community encounter
**Day 6:** Karo community at Korcho cliff (Omo River views); possible ceremony
**Day 7:** Drive to Omorate (Daasanach territory); border with Kenya
**Day 8:** Return north; Key Afer market (if Thursday)
**Day 9:** Drive north to Arba Minch; Nechisar National Park afternoon
**Day 10:** Fly Arba Minch → Addis; depart

# ITINERARY 5: DANAKIL & NORTH CIRCUIT — 9 DAYS

**Slug:** danakil-north-circuit-9-days
**Style:** Frontier Adventure
**Duration:** 9 days
**Price:** From $3,400 per person (2 travelers)

## Overview

The Danakil expedition combined with the northern historic circuit creates one of the most diverse Ethiopia itineraries possible — ancient churches and extreme volcanic landscape within the same journey.

## Day-by-Day (Summary)

**Day 1:** Addis Ababa; evening flight to Mekelle
**Day 2:** Mekelle → Berhale → salt flat caravans → Erta Ale base camp
**Day 3:** Night ascent of Erta Ale lava lake (2–4am); sunrise descent; drive Dallol
**Day 4:** Dallol sulphur formations; return drive to Mekelle
**Day 5:** Mekelle → Lalibela (domestic flight)
**Days 6–7:** Lalibela — full church circuit
**Day 8:** Fly Lalibela → Gondar; afternoon Fasil Ghebbi
**Day 9:** Gondar morning; fly → Addis; depart

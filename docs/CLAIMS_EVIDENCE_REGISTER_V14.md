## Evidence priority before launch

1. Tour-operator certification/license document.
2. Company registration and current legal name.
3. Insurance certificate and scope.
4. Current guide/driver roster and verified experience.
5. Vehicle inventory or supplier agreements.
6. Mobile-camp inventory and configuration photographs.
7. Permission releases for team portraits and biographies.
8. Auditable review/statistics dataset if quantitative social proof will be used.

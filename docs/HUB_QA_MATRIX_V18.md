# V18 Hub Quality Matrix

**Assessment date:** 14 June 2026
**Scope:** implementation quality excluding final photography and live post-deployment field data.
**Important:** these are professional QA scores, not a promise of Google rankings or sales performance.

## Scoring dimensions

- Information architecture
- Content clarity and differentiation
- Decision support and conversion
- Trust and evidence integrity
- SEO metadata and internal discovery
- Structured data and entity clarity
- Accessibility and interaction design
- Performance and rendering strategy
- Maintainability and data integrity
- Mobile/readability readiness

## Remaining non-code boundaries

These do not reduce the V18 implementation score, but they affect the final perceived brand and live commercial outcome:

1. Final licensed photography and video crops
2. More independently verified traveler feedback
3. Publication-ready portraits for founder, guides and drivers
4. Live analytics and real-user Core Web Vitals
5. Production email/DNS credentials and live inbox testing
6. Final legal review of terms, privacy, licence and insurance publication
7. Search-engine indexing and actual ranking performance

No codebase can guarantee a 9/10 visual impression without final media or guarantee search rankings without live competition and field data.

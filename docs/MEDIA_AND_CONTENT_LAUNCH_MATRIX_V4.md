# Media and Content Launch Matrix — V4

## Required final media

| Area | Required asset | Quality rule |
|---|---|---|
| Homepage hero | Sawla Films cinematic 16:9 video + poster | Golden-hour, human, Ethiopia-specific, no stock feel |
| Destination pages | One hero video or strong still per destination | Must show place-specific reality, not generic landscape |
| Itinerary pages | 3–5 supporting images per route | Match route sequence and traveller expectation |
| Field guides | Quiet editorial images or diagrams | Useful, not decorative |
| Species pages | Accurate wildlife images | Species must match the page exactly |
| About/team | Founder/team portraits | Human, warm, credible, field-aware |
| Testimonials | Traveller photos only with permission | Never fake proof |

## File naming convention

Use:

```text
[DESTINATION]_[SUBJECT]_[SOURCE]_[YYYY-MM].jpg
```

Examples:

```text
LALIBELA_BetGiyorgis_SawlaFilms_2026-01.jpg
OMO_HamerMarket_SawlaFilms_2026-02.jpg
BALE_EthiopianWolf_SawlaFilms_2026-03.jpg
```

## Hero video targets

- Desktop: 16:9, 1080p, MP4/WebM, target under 10 MB where possible
- Mobile: 9:16 or cropped vertical-safe MP4/WebM, target under 6 MB
- Always include poster image
- Keep video muted, looped, and non-essential for accessibility

## Do not use

- Generic safari stock
- Over-saturated travel influencer images
- Images that misrepresent access, safety, ceremonies or wildlife proximity
- AI-generated cultural portraits for real communities

## SECTION 7: DEVELOPER IMPLEMENTATION NOTES

### Testimonial card component — reusable

```jsx
<TestimonialCard
  quote="Full quote text here."
  name="Sarah W."
  country="United Kingdom"
  flag="🇬🇧"
  trip="Northern Historic Route"
  initials="SW"
  avatarColor="amber"
/>
```

**Card anatomy:**
- Opening quote mark: `"` at 48px, Teff Gold (#C4931A), decorative only
- Quote: italic, 15px, line-height 1.75, dark charcoal
- Divider: 0.5px solid, border-tertiary
- Avatar row: 48px circle (initials) + name + flag + trip type
- No star ratings on editorial contexts (homepage, About Us)
- Stars optional on tour pages only (if review platform integrated)

### Pull quote component — for Why Travel page
```jsx
<PullQuote
  text="We felt looked after without feeling managed."
  attribution="Sarah W., United Kingdom"
/>
```
Large display treatment: 28–32px, light weight, slight left border in Teff Gold

### Where NOT to repeat the same testimonial
- Sarah W. appears on homepage AND Why Travel — acceptable (different sections, different pages)
- María L. appears on homepage AND 3 other pages — acceptable
- Do not use the same testimonial twice on the same page

### Recommended collection system
After each completed trip, send a personalised email within 7 days:

**Subject:** Your Ethiopia trip — a small favour?

**Body:**
"Hi [Name], thank you for traveling with Sawla Tours. We hope you have wonderful memories from your time in Ethiopia. If you are willing to share a few words about your experience, we would be grateful. Even two or three sentences about what you remember most would be meaningful to us — and to future travelers making the same decision you once made. You can reply to this email directly. We will ask your permission before using anything on our website."

**Response rate tip:** Personal emails from the person who planned their trip outperform automated review requests by 3–4x. Have the planning specialist send it, not a generic account.

### Trustpilot integration (Phase 2)
Once 50+ Trustpilot reviews are collected, replace static testimonials on homepage and tour pages with a live Trustpilot widget or aggregate score badge. Static testimonials remain on About Us (more personal, more curated) and tour pages (trip-specific matching).

## SECTION 9: TESTIMONIAL WRITING STANDARDS

For future testimonial editing (only with explicit client permission):

**Do:**
- Fix spelling and minor grammar silently
- Shorten with "..." if quote is very long
- Remove filler phrases ("like I said," "you know")
- Keep all specific details (places, names, species)

**Do not:**
- Change any substantive word or phrase
- Add words the client did not say
- Combine two separate comments into one quote
- Use a quote without written permission

**Permission record:** Keep a simple spreadsheet with Name, Date of permission, Method (email/WhatsApp), Quote approved, Page(s) used

# Sawla Tours

> Ethiopia's premier boutique tour operator. Tailor-made private journeys since 2009. Based in Addis Ababa.

Sawla Tours specialises in bespoke Ethiopia travel for international clients — heritage pilgrimages, wildlife safaris, tribal encounters, frontier adventures, and festival immersions. We are the only Ethiopian tour operator with in-house documentary production (Sawla Films / ethiopiafilmfixer.com).
